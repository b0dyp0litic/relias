For this project, an eye in as state of discernible emotion, I chose what I feel like should be the only reaction to most of the things that are undulating through our current space and time, absolute terror. A terror imposed, subjugating, subjecting, subsuming, ever-present. A terror you have no place to turn away from or toward because it exists in every direction. A terror where you don’t dare blink, even if you could, because whatever it is waits for your moments of distraction, however brief. An existential terror, a cosmic terror. A terror that is outside of you but also inside of your head. A terror that haunts your unconscious, your waking reality, and everything that lies beyond. That is the only reasonable response to an endless leviathan coiling itself around absolutely everything.
