Control is about your absence of control over virtually every facet of your existence. The inhospitality of systems of control and their inability to be controlled the more and more complex they become. Everything in this game is designed to enforce this dichotomy of control and lack thereof. To reiterate your alienation in the face of the very idea of control. Liminality is the key here, it isn’t an accident that The Backrooms are being released as a film from A24 later this month. This is our world. Most of us behave like the characters in passing in this narrative, acting like the completely absurd and inhospitable are somehow just another day at the office.
