Remedy released the original Alan Wake in 2010 which was a clear build off Twin Peaks but leaned heavier into the weird aspects of a disintegrating universe that allowed in an external force of malevolence. Until Control released and started stitching together the larger world that would birth out the Remedy Extended Universe and forge the throughline between its two major franchises, Alan Wake dropped as a strange, though well received, curiosity with no hard answers, ending, much like Twin Peaks in the 90s did, with a future date to hold out for.
