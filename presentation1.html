The Remedy Universe, Control, The New Weird and The Environment-as-Other - I decided to choose the Remedy universe, but the game Control specifically, as a way to showcase the creative movement known as the New Weird and the imposed alienation-through-environment as an othered entity in-and-of-itself that is used to force the player, reader, viewer, etc. into a state of almost visceral existential isolation and how this is a perfect metaphor for the modern condition.
