I struggled with the patterns at first. I have done plenty of visual work over the years but aside from making a single gift for somebody I don't think I've done pattern work since I was a teenager.

That is when it hit me. Why not just replicate the kind of pattern work I did when I was young. In the nineties I used to make texture pack mods for DOS and early Windows based games like Wolfenstein 3D and Doom...

The first pattern was an analogous color relationship just pushed into high contrast to seem completely unmatchable to real world structures that wouldnt be completely plastic. Intentionally leaning toward orginal NES vibes.
